Berlin, Germany

The beautiful city of Berlin is the capitol of Germany. The city is home to nearly 3.5 Million residents and spands a metropolitan area of 6 Million people. It is rich in culture and history, every turn bringing back some moment in time. Berlin is nestled in the north-east corner of Germany and it's most widely known landmark is Checkpoint Charlie, a marker of past times in the Cold War when Berlin and Germany stood didvided.
